# Buildings

Lands of Ascension has several buildings to help a Legendary Lord Ascend! Each building can be upgraded and it's benefits increase with it's upgrade level.

![Palace](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/939309405227339776/1157086884686737518/Palace-removebg-preview.png)

## Palace

The primary function of the Palace is to provide a Lord with a steady supply golden GWEN coins. GWEN coins can be used to upgrade buildings, hire help, and purchase in-game items.

- The Palace can be upgraded to increase daily GWEN production.
- The Palace can be used to hire Miners that can gather a daily amount of gold related to your Palace's level.
- The Palace can be used to hire Lumberjacks that can gather a daily amount of wood.

![Barracks](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/939309405227339776/1157086885169086534/Hunters_Office-removebg-preview.png)

## Barracks

The primary function of the Barracks is to hire attacks and defenders.

- The Barracks can be upgraded to train better and more plentiful units.
- The Barracks can train Warriors that can raid other Lords' Palaces for resources.
- The Barracks can train Guards that can defend your Palace against a raid.

![Merchant](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/939309405227339776/1157086884447666247/Merchant-removebg-preview.png)
