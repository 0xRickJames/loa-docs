# Game Overview

**[Lands of Ascension](https://landsofascension.quest)** is a city-building simulation game where players embark on a journey to construct and manage their own virtual cities in order to battle in different game modes and potentially earn real world money. The game offers a captivating experience with a focus on resource management, city development, and strategic decision-making.
